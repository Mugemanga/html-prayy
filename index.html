One common challenge in prayer is maintaining consistency (1 Thessalonians 5:17). One of the biggest problems that people encounter is they start with hype and then burn out, fizzle out, or lose the passion that they started off with. In order for that to not happen to you, it’s important to realize that it’s the habits that will get you through, not the hype. Instead of striving for long prayers daily, focus on regular prayer. Make it your aim to have a consistent prayer life that refreshes and blesses you
